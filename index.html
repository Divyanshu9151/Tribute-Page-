Bhagat Singh

Indian Socialist Revolutionary

Photograph of Bhagat Singh taken in 1929

Here's a timeline of Bhagat Singh life:

  • 1907 -Born in Banga Village in the Lyallpur district of the Punjab Province of British India
  • 1919 -Bhagat singh visited Jallianwala Bagh massacre when he was 12 years old
  • 1923 -Bhagat Singh joined National College in Lahore
  • 1924 -Bhagat Singh joined Hindustan Socialist Republican Association
  • 1926 -He founded Indian Socialist Youth organisation Naujawan Bharat Sabha
  • 1927 -He arrested for being involved in bombing that had taken place in Lahore in October 1926
  • 1929 -He started hunger strike in Central jail Mianwali
  • 1929 -His hunger strike has been for more than 116 days
  • 1931 -Bhagat Singh,Rajguru and Sukhdev were setenced to death in Lahore conspiracy case and ordered to be hanged on 24 March

"My life has been dedicated to the noblest cause, that of the freedom of the country.Therefore, there is no rest or worldly desire that can lure me now."

--Bhagat Singh
